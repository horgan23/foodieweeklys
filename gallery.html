Skillet Poutine

What you're gonna need to do is pour a bag of any frozen fries onto a cast iron skillet, next prepare a packet of gravy. After the fries are done, pour the hot gravy onto the fries and then layer with mozzarella cheese and more gravy. Place into the oven for ten more minutes
