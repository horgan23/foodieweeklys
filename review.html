Restaurant Section


Gregg's, Providence RI

While the main food here is subpar, what really shines is their dessert section. What we reccomend would be their chocolate cake because it is rich and decadent,for only $3.99 or if you go on a Tuesday you can get it for free.

Restaurant Section


Giant Oreo Icecream Sandwich - Holy Cow, Glouchester MA

Located at the end of an alleyway, contains a fantastic ice cream parlor. They are affordable and offer reasonable portions.

Restaurant Section


The Baker, New Bedford, MA

A popular coffee shop in New Bedford sells the most decadent pastries you've ever tasted. They are right near the ferry station of Martha's Vineyard so you can load up on sweets.

Restaurant Section


Apple Fig Grilled Cheese - The Common Man, Hooksett NH

Located by the side of a major highway, is a truck stop plaza that has many great sandwiches. My personal favorite is the apple fig grilled cheese because all of the flavors compliment each other very well.
